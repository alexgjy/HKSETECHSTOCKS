Stablecoin & Digital Assets

Sector Analysis

The Hong Kong market for publicly listed stablecoin companies is nascent. As of mid-2025, there are no publicly traded stablecoin issuers on the HKSE. The companies in this sector are primarily licensed Virtual Asset Trading Platforms (VATPs), digital asset-focused financial services firms, and fintech companies utilizing blockchain technology.

Data Clarification: Due to the absence of directly listed stablecoins on the HKSE, the data presented for this sector serves as a proxy, representing key players in the broader 'Digital Asset' and licensed virtual asset trading market segment.
